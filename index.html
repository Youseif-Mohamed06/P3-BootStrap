Toyota GT86 Performance Coupe

Mon. May 25th 2025

The Toyota GT86 is a lightweight rear-wheel-drive sports coupe built for pure driving pleasure. It focuses on balance, sharp handling, and driver control rather than raw horsepower, making it one of the most enjoyable sports cars on the road

⚙️ Key Specifications

  • Engine: 2.0L Naturally Aspirated Boxer
  • Power: 560 HP
  • Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in 3.5 seconds
  • Drivetrain: RWD (Rear-Wheel Drive)
  • Transmission: 6-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic
